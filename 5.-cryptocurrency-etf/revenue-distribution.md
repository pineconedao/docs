# Revenue Distribution

PineconeDAO is committed to providing users with a fair, transparent, and fun way to grow their wealth. We will achieve a reasonable return for platform users through the following revenue distribution strategies:

1. **Lucky Treasure Box Lottery**: Users holding the Mysterious Key NFT are entitled to participate in the Lucky Treasure Box lottery event. Based on the level of the Mysterious Key NFT, users will have a higher chance of winning big prizes. Prizes come from the profits generated by the platform, as well as other donations and sponsorships.
2. **Staking Dividends**: Users who stake PCT tokens in the board contract can receive part of the income generated by the platform. Dividends will be distributed according to the proportion of user-staked PCT, encouraging users to hold and stake PCT for the long term.
3. **Referral Rewards**: To incentivize users to actively invite others to join the PineconeDAO platform, we will provide real-time rewards for inviters. 18% of the donations received will be distributed to the inviting users, encouraging them to invite more people to become members of PineconeDAO.
4. **Mysterious Key NFT**: During the Lucky Treasure Box event, the Mysterious Key NFT may be damaged. Users need to use PCT for repairs. 20% of the repair fees will be burned, and the remaining 80% will be transferred to the board dividend contract for dividends.
5. **Transaction Tax Distribution**: The PCT token transaction tax rate is 5%, of which 50% of the tax revenue will be burned to reduce the circulating supply and create value for token holders; 25% of the tax revenue will be distributed as dividends to the board contract, rewarding users who hold and stake PCT; an additional 25% of the tax revenue will be added to the liquidity pool, ensuring the liquidity of PCT tokens.

{% hint style="info" %}
Through these revenue distribution strategies, PineconeDAO aims to motivate users to actively participate in platform construction and jointly promote the development of Web3.0 technology. We hope to achieve a fair, transparent, and fun way of growing wealth, allowing every user to enjoy investment returns while experiencing the fun brought by PineconeDAO.
